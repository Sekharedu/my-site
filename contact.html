Frequently Asked Questions

What is GST and how does it affect my business?

Goods and Services Tax (GST) is a unified indirect tax on the supply of goods and services. It is crucial for businesses to comply with this tax regime. We assist with GST registration, monthly or quarterly filing, and ensuring all compliance requirements are met.

What accounting services do you provide?

We offer a full range of accounting services, including bookkeeping, financial statement preparation, payroll management, and tax planning. Our goal is to keep your financial records accurate and up-to-date so you can focus on running your business.

Can you help me file my income tax return?

Yes, we provide comprehensive income tax filing services for both individuals and businesses. We ensure all your eligible deductions and exemptions are correctly claimed, helping you maximize your savings and avoid penalties.

What is the process for registering a new company?

The process for company registration can be complex. We guide you through every step, from choosing the right business structure (e.g., sole proprietorship, partnership, private limited) to preparing and submitting all necessary legal documents.

How do I know if my business needs an audit?

The requirement for a statutory audit depends on your business's turnover and legal structure. We can assess your specific situation and advise you on whether an audit is mandatory. We also provide internal audit services to help improve your business's financial health and efficiency.
